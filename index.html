About
Hi! I'm Steven
     ... and I'm a frickin' web master! I'm pretty stoked I can say that. Since the day I dialed-up my first modem nearly 20 years ago, the internet has been an essential part of my life. (I even remember the first thing I did online. It was an Altavista search for wwf.com. Yes, I loved pro wrestling and I didn't understand what an address bar was.) In that time I've seen the web grow from something nerds wasted their time on to the most important technology in the world today. After years of consuming, it's my turn to create. This site is a mere droplet in the vast ocean of information, art, community, and commerce that is the world wide web. Someday soon that droplet will be a puddle. And then maybe a lake, and then... This metaphor sucks. Welcome to my first website.

Google Search Recreation

     A student project that strengthened my HTML and CSS fundamentals. After completing the first part (the Google search bar) I wanted to push the envelope and try to create a near pixel perfect recreation of the Google search results page for one of my favorite athletes, Seattle Seahawks quarterback Russell Wilson. This was my very first project, and completing it gave me the confidence to dive head-first into my newly chosen career. Also, GO HAWKS!
